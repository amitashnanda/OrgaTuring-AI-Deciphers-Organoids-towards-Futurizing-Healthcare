What is OrgaTuring?



OrgaTuring, an artificial intelligence (AI) guided real-time software toolbox that can accurately locate, quantify, track, and classify inflamed vs noninflamed, and healthy vs inflamed Crohn’s disease organoid images. This real-time investigation of human organoids is based on a novel convolutional neural network which is a major AI algorithm. Further, OrgaTuring includes a deep domain adaptation technique to perform classification tasks of a variety of organoid cohorts which vary in terms of molecular and clinical subtypes, and design protocol. Such an approach is first of its kind.
